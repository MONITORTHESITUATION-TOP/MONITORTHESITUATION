Privacy & About

What this site is, who made it, and what it does with your data.

The short version

Monitor The Situation collects zero data. No tracking. No ads. No affiliation fees. It's free, and it will stay free.

Who is behind this

This site is built and maintained by a single individual, as a personal project, for other individual users. There is no company, no team, no investor behind it.

Monitor The Situation is an independent tool created out of a genuine interest in crypto market monitoring. Nothing more.

Data & Privacy

  • No personal data is collected, stored or transmitted.
  • No account or registration is required to use this site.
  • All inputs (token quantities, price targets) are processed locally in your browser only and are never sent to any server.
  • The screenshot feature copies a snapshot of the simulation table to your local clipboard only. Nothing is uploaded.
  • No cookies are set for tracking purposes.
  • No third-party analytics or tracking scripts are loaded.

Business model

  • This site is completely free to use.
  • There are no advertisements.
  • There are no paid affiliate links. Any links to external platforms or projects are purely informational and carry no financial relationship.

External services

Market data is fetched from public APIs (CoinGecko). Charts are rendered via TradingView widgets. These services operate under their own terms and privacy policies. Monitor The Situation does not share any user data with them.
